A Day With Green America

The Life

This is what a Tuesday looks like when you work with your hands, your crew, and gravity.

5:45 AM

The Callout

Dark out. Coffee's already brewed in the thermos. Trucks get loaded — ropes, rigging, saws. Everyone knows their job before a word gets said. The radio crackles. Let's go.

6:30 AM

Rolling Out

Convoy through empty Atlanta streets. Three trucks. The city's still asleep. Somebody's got bad music on and nobody's complaining yet.

7:15 AM

The Climb

First ascent of the day. Spikes bite bark. The world gets quieter the higher you go. At 40 feet, you can see the whole neighborhood. Up here, it's just you, the tree, and the saw.

9:00 AM

Canopy Views

Morning light breaks through the branches. For a second you stop and look around. Hawks circling. Rooftops below. This is the office. Not bad.

11:30 AM

The Cut

The big one. Everything planned. The notch, the hinge, the back cut. Crew's positioned. Ropes tensioned. The saw screams and a 2,000-pound section swings exactly where it should. Clean.

1:00 PM

The Drop

Trunk sections hitting the ground like thunder. The chipper's running. Sawdust everywhere. The homeowner's watching from the porch with their jaw on the floor.

4:30 PM

Cleanup & Load

The yard looks better than before we got there. Trucks loaded. Gear stowed. Handshakes all around.

5:30 PM

Beers After

Because you earned it. The crew at a dive bar, still covered in sawdust. Telling the same stories, laughing at the same jokes. Tomorrow we do it again.

The Work

What We Do

The craft behind the brand.

Tree Removal

Full removals, any size, any access. We've taken 100-foot pines out of backyards with 6 feet of clearance.

Pruning & Trimming

Canopy shaping, deadwood removal, crown reduction. Clean cuts that heal right.

Storm Damage

When it comes down wrong, we come out fast. 24/7 storm response across metro Atlanta.

Stump Grinding

Below grade, clean finish. No stump, no trace, no problem.

Lot Clearing

New builds, overgrown lots, property lines. We clear it clean and haul everything out.

Crane Work

For the big jobs — hazardous leans, tight spaces, structures underneath. Precision extraction.

Emergency Service

Tree on your house at 2 AM? We answer the phone. We show up. That's it.

Consultations

Not sure what you need? We'll come look. Honest assessment, no upsell. Free for Ridealong alumni.
