The Crew

Started With One Climber and a Pickup

Green America Tree Care grew out of a simple idea: show up, do the work right, and let the results speak. We started with one truck, one saw, and a lot of word-of-mouth.

Now we run multiple crews across metro Atlanta — from Buckhead to Marietta, Decatur to Kennesaw. The trucks are bigger, the gear's better, but the approach hasn't changed. We're climbers and tradespeople who take pride in the craft.

Every person on our crew earns their spot. This isn't day-labor work — it's skilled, physical, technical work done by people who've put in the years.
